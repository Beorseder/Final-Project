Your Guide to AP Chemistry

AP Chemistry can seem like a daunting course, but this website is (not) designed to help you understand all the key concepts of AP Chemistry that you need to pass the AP Chemistry exam. Make use of our lessons and practice problems in order to help you learn and understand chemistry to its fullest. Remember that passing this exam can earn you a semester of college credit in chemistry, meaning that you don't have to take an arguably tougher course and you don't have to pay the immense more amount of money that comes with it.
