Big Idea Five

Important Things to Know:

  •       Pauli's Exclusion Principle and how to determine the quantum numbers of any given electron.

  •       Orbital Diagrams and Hund's Rule, Electron Configuration Shorthand, and Noble Gas Electron Configuration Shorthand

  •       Periodic trends of atomic radii, ionic radii, ionization energies, electron affinity, metallic / non-metallic character, and electronegativity.

  •       What Effective Nuclear Charge is, how it is calculated, and what its purple is in determining radii.

  •       How electronegativity affects the overall dipole movement of molecules, affecting their intermolecular forces.

  •       What spectroscopy is, how to read a PES graph, and what the significance of one is.
