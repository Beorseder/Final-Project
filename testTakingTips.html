Test Taking Tips and Strategies

How to Pass the AP Exam:

To ensure that you get that 5 on the AP exam, we want to provide you with all of the best strategies to apply during the test and tips beforehand.

Tips:

  •       Drink lots of water when studying, before the exam, and during the break.

  •       Study Early for the Exam - Try to review the information every few days, starting a couple months before the exam.

  •       Make a studying schedule, especially if you have many AP exams, so that you don't forget one of them.

  •       Emulate a realistic testing environment when practicing, so that you're used to what the exam will feel like.

  •       Make sure to completely understand something, don't just move on if you're unsure about what something means or how it works.

  •       Use AP practice questions from the exam, so that you know what to expect.

Strategies:

  •       Do the easy questions first, then come back to the harder ones. The questions are worth the same amount of points, so answer wisely.

  •       Eliminate answers you know aren't correct. If you have to guess on a question, at least don't guess one of the answers that you know is wrong, make your odds better.

  •       Bring food and water for the break, so that you have lots of energy for the second portion of the exam.

  •       Make sure to keep track of the time you have, so that if there's a question you know will take a long time, you can skip it.
